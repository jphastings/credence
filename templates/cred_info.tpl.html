What does this mean?

Credence is a tool that helps you understand the believability of things online by collecting what people you trust have to say.

The Cred above was made by someone on the internet who wanted to make their trust public.

Running Credence on your machine allows you to choose friends and public figures, and automatically mark their Creds as trustworthy (or ignorable).
